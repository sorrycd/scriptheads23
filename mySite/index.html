About Coleson

Coleson Dyck is a first-year student in Multimedia Productions and a hobbyist in various facets of media creation, such as video editing, graphic design, and light 3D modeling. He initially aspired to work as a police officer, serving in security for four years and spending a year as a Quartermaster and ID Service technician for the Corp. of Commissionaires after graduating from high school. After five years in security, Coleson realized that he was too lazy and unorganized to work in law enforcement, opting instead to pursue something he is more passionate about: design.

Continued...

Coleson enjoys camping and hiking and has been involved with Scouts Canada for 10 years as a participant and for 4 years as a volunteer. Through scouting, he has traveled to both the East and West coasts of Canada, volunteering at events such as the Pacific Jamboree and Canadian Jamboree. For his efforts in food service, he received the “Commissioner's Commendation” award from Scouts Canada. In 2019, he led a group of scouts on a 5-day expedition along the West Coast Trail. Beyond outdoor activities, Coleson is passionate about consuming various forms of media, including movies, games, and trends, and aspires to create his own media to leave a lasting legacy for future generations to enjoy.
