About

A 27-year-old specialist in mergers and acquisitions at an investment firm, Pierce and Pierce, but a “serial killer” at night. The movie details the wacky shenanigans he gets up to in his day-to-day life!

Personality

Unconvicted “serial killer”, petty, greedy, egotistical. Desperate for notoriety while doing nothing to obtain it. He thinks of himself as much more popular than he is.

Skills

“Killing people,” business card design, skin care. He has an incredible aim, dropping a chainsaw from an exceedingly high stairwell. He is excellent at talking his way out of precarious situations, such as his lunch with a detective.
